**Final Works.** The [[Subcontractor]] transfers to the
[[Contractor]] **all intellectual property rights** in all
materials, documentation, and outputs provided to the Contractor
through the performance of Services ([[Deliverables]]) —
**exclusively after full payment** of all fees and costs.

**Open-source code.** The Subcontractor warrants that it has not
included or used, and will not include or use, any Open-Source
Software in the Deliverables without the written consent of the
Contractor.

## When to use this

This variant applies to **non-developer services**: project
management, marketing, consulting, design work that doesn't ship
production code. It's used in:

- `subcontract-pm-2026` (Project Manager)
- `subcontract-marketing-2026` (Marketing)

## Difference from [[IP Assignment]] (dev)

The developer version carries an extended copyleft warning (GPL,
AGPL, "onwards licensing" restrictions) — that's defensive language
against license contamination in the codebase. For PMs and
marketing there's no such surface, so their agreement ships the
shorter form: just "nothing without written consent."

## On acceptance, record

- The deliverable type: document, deck, creative, report
- File format and storage location
- Confirmation of full payment as of the handover date
- That the Deliverable contains no third-party assets (stock
  photos, fonts, music) without proper license
