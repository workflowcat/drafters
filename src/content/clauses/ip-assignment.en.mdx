**Final Works.** The [[Subcontractor]] transfers to the
[[Contractor]] all the intellectual property rights for all the
materials developed for or provided to the Contractor through the
performance of Services ([[Deliverables]]) — **exclusively after
full payment** of all fees and costs.

**Open-source code.** The Subcontractor warrants that it has not
included or used, and will not include or use, any Open-Source
Software or any libraries or code licensed under the General
Public License (GPL), AGPL or anything similar, where such license
would require our Software to be licensed onwards, without prior
written consent of the Contractor.

## Critical points

- **"Exclusively after full payment"** is the phrase some
  subcontractors ask to remove. Don't remove it without legal
  consultation — it's our leverage in a dispute.
- GPL/AGPL in our codebase can force us to open-source the entire
  product it's integrated into. For commercial work this is fatal.
- MIT / Apache 2.0 / BSD — usually OK. But verify with legal per
  project.
- On delivery, always record the fact of full payment — that's the
  moment IP transfers.
