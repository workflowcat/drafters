**Payment Dispute.** If any amount claimed as payable is disputed in
good faith, the [[Contractor]] shall pay the undisputed portion and
give a **5 business days** notice specifying the basis of the dispute
in reasonable detail.

Payments that are undisputed by the Contractor within 5 business days
of receipt are considered accepted.

## Things to watch

- **5 business days** is short. If your approval flow runs longer,
  negotiate 10 days.
- "Undisputed … considered accepted" is a silent-acceptance rule.
  If you miss the window, you pay.
- This clause works together with [[Rate Increase]] and the Invoicing
  row in the Key Terms table.
