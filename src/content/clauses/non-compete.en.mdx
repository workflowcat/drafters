During the term of this agreement and for **1 year after its
termination**, the [[Subcontractor]] may not engage in any business
activity with persons who were clients of the [[Contractor]] and
whose existence the Subcontractor became aware of while performing
this agreement.

## Notes

- Protects against "the subcontractor learned about a client
  through us and went direct."
- The restriction applies ONLY to clients the subcontractor learned
  about in our engagement. Their other clients are their business.
- Together with [[Non-solicitation]], these two clauses form the
  full commercial-relationship perimeter.
- In the EU and US, restrictions must be **reasonable in time and
  geography** — 1 year is the safe upper bound.
