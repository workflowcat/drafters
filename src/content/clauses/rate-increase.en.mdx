**Rate increase.** The [[Subcontractor]] may increase [[Rate|rates]]
no more than once every **12 months**, and the increase shall not
exceed **10% annually**.

## Notes

- Rate changes are not automatic. The subcontractor initiates them
  in writing.
- We may decline a proposed increase — the parties then negotiate or
  terminate at the existing rate.
- Our client-facing Services Agreement typically caps at 12%; the
  subcontract caps at 10%. That's deliberate — it protects margin.
- If the rate is renegotiated "to market" without a cap, we lose
  control over project unit economics.
