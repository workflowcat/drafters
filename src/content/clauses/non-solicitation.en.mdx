**Non-solicitation period.** During the term of this agreement and
for **1 year after its termination**, neither party shall solicit,
hire or encourage the departure of any person who is an employee,
contractor or subcontractor of the other party.

**Inbound job requests.** If any such person **independently**
approaches the other party about employment, that party shall
immediately suspend the request and notify the first party.

## Notes

- **1 year post-term** is our standard. In some jurisdictions
  (California, parts of the EU) these restrictions can be
  unenforceable.
- "Independently requests" creates transparency but is hard to
  monitor in practice — it operates as a moral obligation.
- A subcontractor wishing to join us full-time isn't forbidden, but
  it must go through explicit notification and joint approval.
