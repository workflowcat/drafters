**Whereas** the Customer desires to engage the Contractor to provide

basis, and the Contractor agrees to provide such services subject
to the terms below.

## 1. Key Terms

| Parameter | Value |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Service provided** | Software development and IT consulting (hereinafter the "**Services**") |
| **Billing model** | **Time & Materials** — monthly billing based on actual hours worked |
| **Invoicing** | Monthly, within **5 business days** after the end of each calendar month. The Customer must pay within **15 calendar days** from the invoice date. |
| **Time tracking** | The Contractor provides detailed time reports with each invoice, showing hours per team member and a brief description of work performed. |
| **Payment transfers** | To the banking account specified in the invoice. The sending party covers bank charges. |
| **Term and Termination** | At-will termination with **30 days** prior notice. |
| **Team** | The Contractor may engage subcontractors. |
| **Overtime** | Work beyond 40 hours/week per team member requires prior written approval. Overtime rate = **150%** of the standard hourly rate. |
| **Rate increase** | No more than once every 12 months. Not more than **12%** annually, with **60 days** prior written notice. |

## 2. Scope of Services

The Contractor shall provide the Services as described in Attachment A
(Rate Sheet) and any subsequent Statements of Work agreed in writing.
The Customer directs the work through a designated point of contact.

Services are provided on an **effort basis** — the Contractor commits
to providing qualified personnel and reasonable diligence, but does
not guarantee specific outcomes or deliverables unless separately
agreed in a Statement of Work.
