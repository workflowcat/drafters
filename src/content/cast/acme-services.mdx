## Acme Services GmbH

**Acme** is the contractor side of every engagement in this library —
a Berlin-headquartered software consulting shop that sells
time-and-materials engineering support to mid-market and enterprise
clients across the EU and UK. The legal entity is a GmbH under German
law; individual deals are usually governed by the client's preferred
forum or by England and Wales as a neutral international choice.

## Commercial shape

- **Default billing:** monthly in arrears, 14-day payment terms.
  Accepts 30 days for enterprise clients; anything beyond 45 days is
  a red flag that AP dictates, not the engagement sponsor.
- **Rate posture:** review at most once per 12 months, capped at 10%.
  Typical increase is 5–7%.
- **Engagement model:** MSA + SoW. Each engagement gets its own SoW;
  the MSA carries IP, liability, confidentiality, governing law.
- **Overtime:** 2× rate, written approval only. Rarely invoked; the
  team manages sprint-level load internally.

## Who Acme engages with

On the **client side**, Acme sells directly to engineering-led
mid-market ([[Horizon]]), aggressive US healthtech (Kestrel),
committee-driven enterprise (Helvetic), and the occasional slow-pay
media holding (Aria).

On the **subcontractor side**, Acme engages specialists for PM and
marketing when client load runs ahead of internal capacity. Those
engagements live under separate subcontract templates.
